# Easter Egg & Delight Details

Rules for playful details, hidden features, and injecting personality into interfaces.

## Rules

### Meaningful icon details
Embed real, meaningful details in your app icon as a hidden Easter egg (e.g., Apple's Mail icon contains a real address). Rewards curious users and signals craftsmanship.

### Cultural text in icons
Include meaningful or iconic text content in text-editor or document app icons. Turns the icon into a cultural artifact and a talking point.

### Footer surprises
Add a subtle interactive Easter egg in your website footer. Footers are the least-visited part of a page — a surprise there rewards the users who explore furthest.

### Credits roll
Add a movie-credits-style "cast" section at the end of your landing page listing the team. Humanizes the product and gives the team visible credit.

### Interactive 404 pages
Transform your 404 page into an interactive, playful experience rather than a dead end. A 404 is an opportunity — turning an error into delight changes the user's emotional state.

### Personality in error pages
Add personality and interactivity to error pages to soften the frustration of encountering them. Few users see error pages, but those who do are already frustrated — reduce their pain.

### Hidden developer Easter eggs
Hide a small visual Easter egg (like a golden thumb) in developer or inspect tools. Delights the power users who spend the most time in your product.

### Meet the maker
Include a hidden "Meet the Developer" feature or page accessible through a non-obvious gesture. Creates a personal connection between makers and users.

### Randomized hover emoji
Randomly cycle the emoji icon each time the user hovers over an emoji button. A near-zero-cost detail that adds playful unpredictability to a routine interaction.

### Activity visualization
Visualize the user's activity across workspaces or projects on an interactive map or timeline. Transforms usage data into a visual narrative that users enjoy exploring.

### Live text in images
Use the system's live text recognition to enable highlighting and interaction with text in images. Blurs the line between static images and interactive text.

### Memorable invite codes
Format invite codes as color names with hex values instead of random strings. Transforms a mundane string into something memorable and delightful.
