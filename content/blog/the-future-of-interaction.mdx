The web is evolving rapidly. We are moving away from passive information consumption towards fully immersive, cinematic interactive experiences.

With standard APIs like WebGL and WebGPU becoming ubiquitously robust, the barrier to high-end rendering in the browser has vanished. 

## Brutalism Meets Interaction

Brutalism in digital design is often misunderstood as unpolished. True modern brutalism relies heavily on:
1. **Raw Typography**: Fonts pushing boundaries in scaling and weight.
2. **Minimal Palettes**: Often monochromatic, leveraging grayscale contrast.
3. **Hyper-Focus**: Removing distractions, leaving only purposeful pixels.

When we couple this stark visual aesthetic with fluid, high-frame-rate interaction powered by libraries like `GSAP` and `Lenis` smooth scrolling, a new paradigm emerges.

> "A design isn't finished when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away." — Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

// Example of fluid velocity-based scaling in GSAP

A seamless experience is born in the micro-adjustments taking place every frame. The intersection of motion and structure creates a narrative thread that pulls users linearly through a domain. The future is kinetic.
